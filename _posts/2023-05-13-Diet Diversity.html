Tadarida brasiliensis

Tadarida brasiliensis (italics), commonly known as the Brazilian Free-tailed bats, are small, insectivorous bats that are famously known to eat lots of pests. These bats range from the northern tips of Oregon deep into South America.

During the summer months, females form enormous maternal roosts where they rear their pups
